Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026  |  Brad Norman — Sole Proprietor

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Brad Norman ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our SMS notification service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. SMS Notification Service

Our SMS service sends automated flight opportunity alerts to subscribed mobile phone numbers. Messages are triggered by monitored data sources and are used solely to notify subscribers of relevant flight opportunities.

3. Information We Collect

When you subscribe via our opt-in form, we collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • The date and time you opted in or opted out

This information is stored securely on our servers and used only to send you the alerts you subscribed to.

4. How We Use Information

The sole purpose of collecting your phone number is to deliver the automated SMS alerts you requested. No data is used for marketing, advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond delivering the notifications for which you subscribed.

5. SMS Opt-In and Opt-Out

You provide explicit consent to receive SMS alerts by completing the opt-in form and checking the consent checkbox. You may discontinue SMS notifications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message
  • Visiting our unsubscribe page

Reply HELP for assistance. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any personal information with third parties. Phone number opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio solely to deliver SMS messages on our behalf.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect subscriber data. Phone numbers and opt-in records are stored on secured servers with access limited to this service.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
