Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026  |  Brad Norman — Sole Proprietor

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Brad Norman ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our SMS notification service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. SMS Notification Service

Our SMS service sends personal notification alerts to the account owner's own registered mobile phone number. Messages are triggered automatically by an email webhook and are used solely to notify the account owner of incoming emails. No third parties receive these messages.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal data from end users. The only phone number used in this service is the account owner's own number, which is configured directly within the service and is not submitted through any public-facing form.

4. How We Use Information

The sole purpose of this service is to deliver automated SMS alerts to the account owner. No data is used for marketing, advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond delivering the notification for which it was triggered.

5. SMS Opt-In and Opt-Out

Because this service sends messages exclusively to the account owner, consent is implicit in the configuration of the service. The account owner may discontinue SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message or by disabling the service. Reply HELP for assistance.

Message frequency varies based on email volume. Standard message and data rates may apply.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any personal information with third parties. Phone number opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party for marketing or promotional purposes.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any configuration data used to operate this service. No sensitive personal data is stored on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
